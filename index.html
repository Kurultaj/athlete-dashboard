Phase 3 intro (2027)
Endurance ride (Zone 2)
60–90 min conversational pace. Cadence 80–90 rpm. Road bike or indoor trainer. 2x/week.
2 x 60–90 min/week
Brick workout (bike to run)
60 min easy bike immediately followed by 15–20 min easy run. Trains the quad-to-glute muscle recruitment shift at T2. The brick legs feeling disappears after 4–6 sessions.
1x/week from Phase 3
Ironman bike caution
Stay Zone 2–3 on the bike even when feeling strong. Going too hard on the 180 km leg is the number one cause of Ironman DNF and run collapse.

multi-year plan — marathon to ironman
1
Phase 1 — Foundation
Strength + swim base
Jun 2026 to Oct 2026
  • Gym 3x/week (Pull/Push/Legs) — strength base protects all future phases
  • Run 2x/week: easy Zone 2 + long run building to 20 km
  • Swim 2x/week: technique drills (head position, high-elbow catch, bilateral breathing)
  • Double progression on all gym lifts — add reps before weight
  • Strength before cardio on any same-day sessions
2
Phase 2 — Marathon block
First race — winter 2026
Oct to Dec 2026 — ~13 weeks
  • Running becomes primary: easy + tempo + long run (builds to 32 km)
  • Gym drops to 2x/week maintenance: 3 sets, injury prevention priority
  • Keep RDL + single-leg calf raise as non-negotiable injury prevention
  • Gut-train with gels every 45 min from 75 min onward on long runs
  • 3-week taper: volume down 30–40%, intensity maintained
3
Phase 3 — Triathlon entry
Sprint to Olympic to 70.3
Jan 2027 to Dec 2027
  • Add cycling: 2x/week Zone 2 + 1x brick workout (bike to run)
  • Sprint triathlon (750 m / 20 km / 5 km) — Q2 2027
  • Olympic triathlon (1.5 km / 40 km / 10 km) — Q3/Q4 2027
  • Swim 3x/week, build to 2,000–2,500 m sessions, open water monthly
  • Ironman 70.3 target: late 2027 or early 2028 as qualifier
  • Gym 2x/week maintenance only
4
Phase 4 — Ironman
Full distance — 2028 to 2030
2028 to race day
  • 8–18 hrs/week peak training: 5–6 days, sometimes 2 sessions/day
  • Bike dominant: ~50% of total training volume
  • Swim 3–4x/week: 3,000–4,000 m sessions, open water, drafting, sighting
  • Gym 2x/week: injury prevention, not hypertrophy
  • Minimum 24 weeks specific preparation for first full Ironman
  • Taper 4–6 weeks out — volume drops, intensity maintained
